Kyrie 6
Shredding defenses with speed, Kyrie Irving attacks from all angles using an explosive first step and impossible-to-guard-moves. The Kyrie 6 enhances his on-court acceleration with a large Nike Air Zoom Turbo unit in the forefoot and curved outsole for precision quickness.

Is Kyrie 6 good for outdoor?
It shouldn't matter what type of footwork you have, the traction on the Kyrie 6 is everywhere, and should remain in contact with the ground no matter what. The rubber is a little soft for outdoor use, but if outdoor basketball is all you're able to play then you'll at least have great traction while it lasts.
